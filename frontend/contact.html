Frequently Asked Questions

How does SkillMatch work?

SkillMatch uses advanced AI to analyze your resume and job descriptions, extracting skills and calculating compatibility scores. It then searches multiple job boards to find relevant opportunities that match your profile.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All uploads are processed securely, and we never share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent.

What file formats are supported?

We support PDF, DOC, and DOCX formats for both resumes and job descriptions. Maximum file size is 10MB per document.

How accurate are the match scores?

Our AI algorithms are continuously trained and improved to provide highly accurate match scores. However, match scores should be used as a guide alongside your own judgment when evaluating job opportunities.
